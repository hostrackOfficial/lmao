Our seamless process

Our goal is to make the entire process as smooth and hassle-free as possible, so you can focus on running your business while we take care of the landing page for you.

1-6

Kick-off call

This is our first meeting where we discuss your project and make sure we are all on the same page before we get started.

2-6

Onboarding

The step right after our kick-off call. We use an onboarding form to gather some information about your business and what you are looking for in your project. This helps us make sure we are meeting your needs. We also add you to a dedicated Slack channel for the project.

3-6

Wireframing

Wireframing is a way for us to map out the structure and layout of your landing page. It's kind of like a blueprint for how everything will fit together.

4-6

Design

Design is a huge aspect of creating a good-performing landing page. We'll work with you to create a style that fits your brand and meets your goals.

5-6

Development

Development is the process of actually building your landing page. This is where we take all of the planning and design and turn it into a functioning product.

6-6

Hand-off

The hand-off is when we give you the finished product and all of the necessary materials. This is where the real magic starts. Get ready to see your business elevate to the next level.

What is a landing page and why do I need one?

A landing page is a focused web page designed for a specific marketing goal, like signing up or buying. You need one to boost conversions by keeping the message clear and distractions minimal.

How much does it cost to create a landing page?

The cost varies depending on complexity. Simple ones may be around $100-$300, while more advanced pages can range from $500 to $5,000 or more. Factors include design, functionality, copywriting, and integrations. It's best to discuss your specific needs for an accurate quote.

How long does it take to create a landing page?

The time to create a landing page can vary based on complexity and requirements. A simple landing page might take a few hours to a day, while a more complex one with custom design and features may take a week or more.

Do you offer any guarantees for the performance of my landing page?

While we can't provide specific performance guarantees, our team will optimize your landing page and offer recommendations aligned with industry best practices to enhance its effectiveness.

Do you offer ongoing support for my landing page?

Yes, we provide ongoing support for your landing page, including updates, maintenance, and assistance with optimizations as needed.
